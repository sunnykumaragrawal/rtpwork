About Dr. RTP Team

Rtp Medical Solution

We are a leading medical solutions expert in the United States.

The idea behind RTP Medical Solution was to develop a long term concept which aims to improve people’s medical care.

  • In 2014, we wanted a simple and affordable solution to collect reviews for paediatrics. Over the past years, RTP medical solutions evolved into a platform that handles advanced features like appointment booking and payment systems, electronic forms, surveys, call list management, and more.
  • It quickly became clear that both patients and practitioners were responsive to this innovative tool. We permanently think critically about our service to ensure it always fulfils both the demands of health professionals and the patients’ expectations.
